# 🚖 Uber NCR Analytics Dashboard

Built an end-to-end Power BI dashboard to analyze Uber ride bookings across Delhi NCR and uncover insights around revenue, ride performance, cancellations, customer satisfaction, and location-based demand.

This project focuses on turning raw operational data into actionable business insights through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

## 📊 Key Metrics

- 150K Total Bookings
- ₹51.85M Total Revenue
- 62% Completion Rate
- ₹4.59M Lost Revenue
- 176 Pickup Locations

## 🔍 What the Dashboard Covers

- Revenue and profitability analysis
- Ride completion and cancellation trends
- Vehicle-wise performance comparison
- Customer and driver rating analysis
- Peak booking hour identification
- Delhi NCR geospatial demand analysis

## 💡 Key Findings

- Auto and Go Mini were the strongest revenue contributors.
- Driver-side cancellations had a significant impact on revenue loss.
- Ride demand peaked during evening hours.
- A small number of locations generated a disproportionately high share of bookings and revenue.
